LAKKI HISTORY

The lake 'LAKKI' is formerly known as Leykk and was later on changed and renamed after a handsome man visited the place and impreganated almost every women on the lake. After such incident, the man had vanished and is nowhere to be seen. Each women had hoped that he would return for the second round. But unfortunately, after years of waiting. There has been no signs of the man's return. Hence, in his memory, the lake had been named 'LAKKI' to remind all the women that there had been once a man that brought them to heaven.
